DroneDeploy is a leading drone software platform for construction, providing aerial mapping, progress tracking, and site analysis solutions to help construction companies build more efficiently.

TASK:
Classify the following company and speaker role into one of five categories based on their potential relationship to DroneDeploy:

CLASSIFICATION LOGIC:

CRITICAL: You MUST follow these steps in order. Step 1 is ABSOLUTE and overrides all other considerations.

Step 1 - Company Classification (CHECK THIS FIRST):
- COMPETITOR: Companies that provide drone mapping, construction software, or competing technology solutions. This includes:
  * ALWAYS COMPETITOR: Autodesk, Bentley, Bentley Systems, Trimble, PlanGrid, Procore, Procore Technologies, Pix4D, Skycatch, DroneSeed, Kespry, Measure, Site 1001, Propeller Aero

  * If the company name contains or is affiliated with these competitors, classify as COMPETITOR regardless of the person's role

  * Cloud infrastructure providers: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, Microsoft
  * System integrators or IT consultancies
  * Hardware/sensor manufacturers (not software)
  * IMPORTANT: If uncertain whether Partner or Competitor, classify as COMPETITOR

- If NOT Competitor or Partner, continue to Step 2

Step 2 - Role Classification (ONLY if Step 1 determined NOT Competitor/Partner):
Evaluate the speaker's company type:

  * Examples: Real estate developers, property owners, facility management companies
  * Job titles: Real Estate Developer, Facility Manager, Asset Manager, Owner's Representative, Development Manager, CIO (if at owner organization)

- OTHER: Academic, government, consultant, unclear roles, or roles that don't fit Builder/Owner categories
